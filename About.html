New owners of The Plough in New Romney

Dean and Becky wish to welcome you to their new local venture. They recently bought The Plough outright and want to build on the already popular venue to make it the best family-friendly pub in the area.

A new children's room is being added where kids can play safely with the new play equipment...more on this soon.

The Pub will close for one week from the 4th October until the 8th October for essential works and redecorating.

Reopening on the 9th October with a party, live bands will be playing from 2pm through into the evening.

We look forward to welcoming the local community when we open the doors.

Thank you and see you soon!
